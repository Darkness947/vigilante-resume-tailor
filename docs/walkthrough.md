# VIGILANTE Operational Walkthrough

Welcome to the **VIGILANTE Resume Tailor**. This guide delineates the standard user pathway, mapping out exactly how you transition from an un-optimized raw file to a highly competitive ATS asset.

## 1. Perimeter Access
1.  **Landing Protocol**: Navigate to the VIGILANTE Base URL. The system will welcome you with high-contrast mission briefings.
2.  **Authentication**: Attempting to interact with the Tailor immediately demands identity verification. Pass through the secure Auth forms, providing email and establishing a password structure to claim your Supabase RLS instance.

## 2. The Core Tailoring Workflow
Once localized within the **Dashboard**, you manipulate the engine settings:
1.  **Asset Configuration**: Drop your `.pdf` or `.docx` into the secure parser target zone.
2.  **Target Designator**: Provide the explicit Job Title and extensive Job Description you are aggressively targeting.
3.  **Intensity Allocation**: Dial in the `Tailoring Strength` — select between *Conservative* tone adjustments and *Aggressive* structural rewrites.
4.  **Language Targeting**: Switch universally from `en` (English) to `ar` (Arabic) depending on your target territory.

## 3. Protocol Compilation
Click **"TAILOR RESUME"**.
*   VIGILANTE strips the data bare and connects to Google Gemini APIs.
*   The system meticulously scores the incoming data, extracting absent keywords based purely on your target's designator.
*   The React-PDF Renderer sews the intelligent JSON fragments into a flawless structure.

## 4. History & Asset Retrieval
Your generated document is not ephemeral.
- Open the **Protocol Registry (History)** tab from your user menu.
- VIGILANTE secures every single execution cleanly, detailing your exact generated ATS score against the Target Job.
- If you activated **Email Intercepts** in your profile, expect a direct transmission containing a secure, 2-hour encrypted URL directly granting the PDF artifact in your inbox!
