# 🚀 VIGILANTE Operational Walkthrough

Welcome to the **VIGILANTE Resume Tailor**. This guide delineates the standard user pathway, mapping out exactly how you transition from an un-optimized raw file to a highly competitive ATS asset.

---

## 🛡️ Phase 1: Perimeter Access & Identity

### 1.1 Landing Protocol
Navigate to the VIGILANTE Base URL. The system will welcome you with a high-contrast mission briefing and a breakdown of current ATS vulnerabilities.
- **Action**: Review the "Features" section to understand the engine's capabilities.

### 1.2 Secure Authentication
Attempting to interact with the Tailor immediately demands identity verification.
- **Step**: Click "Get Started".
- **Step**: Enter your credentials or use a Magic Link.
- **Result**: Upon successful auth, you are granted a unique UUID and a private slice of the Supabase PostgreSQL database.

---

## 🧠 Phase 2: The Core Tailoring Workflow

Once localized within the **Dashboard**, you have full control over the AI engine.

### 2.1 Asset Induction (The Upload)
Drop your `.pdf` or `.docx` into the secure parser target zone.
> [!TIP]
> Ensure your resume is clean of complex graphics for the most accurate AI parsing.

### 2.2 Target Designator (The Job)
Provide the explicit Job Title and the full Job Description. 
- **The Secret Sauce**: The more detailed the JD, the more "lethal" the AI optimization becomes.

### 2.3 Intensity Allocation (The "Vigilante" Mode)
Dial in the `Tailoring Strength`:
- **Conservative**: Professional polishing, minor tone adjustments.
- **Standard**: Balanced rewriting, keyword optimization.
- **Aggressive**: Structural overhaul, heavy impact-verb injection, and tactical data repositioning.

### 2.4 Language Targeting
Switch universally from `en` (English) to `ar` (Arabic). The UI will flip to RTL (Right-to-Left) mode instantly.

---

## ⚙️ Phase 3: Protocol Compilation

Click **"TAILOR RESUME"**. Here is what happens under the hood:
1. **Extraction**: VIGILANTE strips the document into raw text.
2. **Analysis**: Connects to Google Gemini 3.1 Pro APIs.
3. **Scoring**: Calculates your current match score vs. the target job.
4. **Optimization**: Re-writes your professional summary and experience bullets.
5. **Synthesis**: The React-PDF Renderer compiles the new JSON into a pristine PDF.

---

## 📂 Phase 4: History & Asset Retrieval

Your generated documents are permanently secured.

### 4.1 Protocol Registry (History)
Open the **History** tab to see all previous missions.
- **Metrics**: View the ATS score achieved for each generation.
- **Downloads**: Re-download any previous PDF instantly.

### 4.2 Email Intercepts
If you activated **Email Notifications** in your settings:
- VIGILANTE will blast a secure, 2-hour encrypted URL directly to your inbox.
- This allows for "on-the-go" retrieval without needing to log back into the dashboard.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting & Support

- **Parsing Error**: Ensure the file is not password protected.
- **AI Timeout**: Large resumes (>5 pages) may take up to 45 seconds. Do not refresh.
- **Styling Glitches**: Clear your browser cache to ensure the latest Tailwind v4 tokens are loaded.
